Welcome to Life Soundtrack

Enter a prompt into our soundtrack generator to begin.

  • Example 1: “Reinvention with Upbeat Electronic Music” – Fresh, energetic, risk-taking vibes.
  • Example 2: “Leaving home for the first time” – Must include “Time of my Life” by David Cook.
  • Example 3: “30-min commute as a space bandit escape” – some instrumental allowed.

After creating a soundtrack, album art cards will appear.
If you want to iterate, long-click (or Shift-click) up to 5 cards, then press “Create Soundtrack” again.
